01. About me

I’ve always loved the idea of building things; in the world and virtually. I have a Masters degree in Quantity Surveying: building and developing does not get realer than that.

But I’ve also loved creating websites since I first started customising Blogger sites in the late 2000s. After WordPress was launched as a competition, it was just a matter of months before I was paying for wordpress.org and creating websites using all the free themes at first, and then the paid themes.

After years of creating WordPress-based websites, I want to understand and create a career out of how the structure works from the foundation to the final colour stroke on the exterior. I want to be able to experience and create web experiences that go beyond a single CMS. That’s why I’m doing this.

Some of the technologies I’ve worked with and have started recently working with:

  • WordPress (of course)
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Git, GitHub and GitLab
